This project focuses on analyzing a supermarket sales dataset to uncover meaningful insights related to customer behavior, product performance, revenue trends, and branch-level operations. The dataset includes detailed transactional information such as invoice IDs, branch and city data, customer demographics (gender and customer type), product categories, pricing, quantity purchased, taxes, totals, payment methods, gross income, and customer ratings.

The objective of the project is to clean, explore, and interpret the data in order to:

*Key Goals

Examine sales performance across different branches and cities.

Analyze product line popularity and revenue contribution.

Understand customer demographics and their purchasing behavior.

Identify peak sales times and trends using timestamp data.

Explore relationships between pricing, quantity sold, gross income, and customer satisfaction.

Create visualizations to clearly communicate sales patterns and business insights.

*Expected Outcomes

A comprehensive analysis report covering patterns and insights.

Data visualizations (graphs and charts) for better interpretation.

Recommendations to improve business strategy, inventory planning, and customer engagement.

A cleaned and well-structured dataset ready for advanced modeling or machine-learning tasks (optional).
